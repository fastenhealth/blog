Don’t make patients prove themselves twice. 

Fasten’s 𝐁𝐫𝐢𝐧𝐠 𝐘𝐨𝐮𝐫 𝐎𝐰𝐧 𝐈𝐝𝐞𝐧𝐭𝐢𝐭𝐲 capability is now live! 

Fasten lets patients share records without completing identity proofing a second time when your organization uses a supported Credential Service Provider like CLEAR, [ID.me](http://ID.me), or Persona.

That means your team can keep the identity stack you already use.

Patients verify identity in your product first, and when they’re ready to share records, Fasten uses that verified identity to continue the record retrieval flow.

𝘕𝘰 “𝘥𝘪𝘥𝘯’𝘵 𝘐 𝘢𝘭𝘳𝘦𝘢𝘥𝘺 𝘥𝘰 𝘵𝘩𝘪𝘴?” 

Teams shouldn’t have to replace their identity stack just to enable medical record access. 𝐁𝐫𝐢𝐧𝐠 𝐘𝐨𝐮𝐫 𝐎𝐰𝐧 𝐈𝐝𝐞𝐧𝐭𝐢𝐭𝐲 lets record retrieval fit into the product and patient journey you’ve already built.

For implementation details, the full technical guide is here: [https://docs.connect.fastenhealth.com/identity-proofing/introduction](https://docs.connect.fastenhealth.com/identity-proofing/introduction)
